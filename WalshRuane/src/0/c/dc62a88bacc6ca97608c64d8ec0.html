Find A Grave Gravesite Information

Publication information https://www.findagrave.com

Narrative

Find A Grave has two kinds of information, gravestone inscriptions and memorials that are added to the site (information not found at the gravesite, such as deatils about the deceased persons live and family). As gravestone inscription information is generally considered to be correct and memorials can contain unverified information there are two separate Sources for Find A Grave information in this tree. This will allow readers to better determine the reliablility of the information referenced.

"Find A Grave Gravesite Information" is a source only for gravestone inscriptions, not for memorials added to the site.

"Find A Grave Memorials" is a source only for memorials (meaning information about the person not found at the gravesite).

Narrative

Find a Grave's mission is to find, record and present final disposition information from around the world as a virtual cemetery experience.

Memorial contributions to Find A Grave must fulfill that mission - registration of the final disposition. If the memorial contribution corresponds with only the main mission, then the memorial fulfills its purpose as part of Find A Grave's mission.

Find a Grave memorials may contain rich content including pictures, biographies and more specific information. Members can leave remembrances via 'virtual flowers' on the memorials they visit, completing the virtual cemetery experience.
