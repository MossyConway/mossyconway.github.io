Irish Births 1864-1958

Author FindMyPast
Publication information http://search.findmypast.ie/search-world-Records/irish-births-1864-1958

Narrative

Discover if your ancestor was born in Ireland between 1864 and 1958. The records will reveal your relative’s full name, birth year, and the quarter and district they were registered in. Details such as your relative’s mother’s maiden name will allow you to delve further back into your family history.

What can these records tell me?
Each record is a transcript of the original birth record. The amount of information included varies, but the records usually include the following details about your ancestor:

• First name
• Last name
• Year
• Registered quarter
• Registration district
• Country
• Mother’s maiden name (only available after 1929)

This collection differs from "Civil Birth Records, Ireland" [ https://civilrecords.irishgenealogy.ie ] in the followings ways:

• There are no images accompanying the transcripts in this collection, so you cannot get full record, only the information that was transcribed
• This collection includes records up to 1958 whereas "Civil Birth Records, Ireland" can at most only provide records for up to one hundred years ago
