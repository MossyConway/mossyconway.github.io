Cleary Family History Report

Author South Mayo Family Research Centre & Jim Fritch
Publication information https://www.ancestry.co.uk/family-tree/tree/79749035/media

Narrative

The Cleary Family History Report documents the families and descendants of a number landholders with the surname Cleary living in Cossallagh, Lurgan and Ballymangan in Mayo in the 1800s.

The original report was created for Created for T. J. Cleary of London in 2001, this Thomas a descendant of John Cleary b.~1819, husband of Honor Coyne.

Jim Fritch significantly expanded the report by adding information from various people and resources.

Tom Sullivan and Tomás Conway have aded to Jim's version of the report.

The most recent revisoin of the report can be found in the Media section of Tom Sullivan's Ancestry.com tree : Cleary's of Redhill, Mayo, Ireland
