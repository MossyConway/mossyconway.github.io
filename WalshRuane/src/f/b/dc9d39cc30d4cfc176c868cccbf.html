Narrative

This source is a transcription by Janet Payne of the microfilmed Knock and Aghamore parish records at a Family History Centre of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

This is information about the microfilms can be found here: https://www.familysearch.org/search/catalog/423429?availability=Family%20History%20Library and https://www.familysearch.org/search/catalog/430520?availability=Family%20History%20Library

The following information is from http://eastmayo.org where Janet published her transcriptions. Note that that website no longer exists, but a copy of the information can found on https://web.archive.org/web/20110113205923/http://www.eastmayo.org:80/ :

KNOCK (AGHAMORE) PARISH RECORDS & KNOCK PARISH RECORDS

BAPTISMS
The Catholic parish of Knock contains the civil parish of Knock.
The LDS film number for the Knock (Aghamore) and Knock parish Baptism records is 1279206.
The first Baptismal transcriptions are for Aghamore, 1864-1883 inclusive.
The second set of transcriptions are for Knock, 1869-1905 inclusive.

The “-sp-“ following the parents’ names means sponsors or Godparents.

These records are not complete, and many errors exist due to the very poor quality of the film. Many of the entries were not transcribed as they are illegible. I interpreted the handwriting to the best of my ability, and many errors were undoubtedly made. When searching the records, remember that many times the original transcriber spelled the names phonetically, and are not necessarily spelled as you may think. Many of the surnames had multiple variant spellings, so is best to search all variations. Therefore, you should use this information as a guide only and if you think you find an ancestor, you should always refer back to the film itself.
