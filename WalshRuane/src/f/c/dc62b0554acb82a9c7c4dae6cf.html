Indexes and images

In order for you to be able to find records of your ancestors online someone first has to read the original handwritten records (or photographs of the records) and type up the information on a computer. This process is called indexing . There are then two formats in which you can get the record information: Indexes which have information copied from the original record and images (photographs) of the original record. An index will typically have a subset of the information that can be found in the full record, for example this is the Civil Death Record index for John Smith d1864 -

Name JOHN SMITH
Year of Death 1864
 Group Registration ID N/R
SR District/Reg Area Dublin South
Deceased Age at Death 78
Returns Year 1864
Returns Quarter 2
Returns Volume No 7
Returns Page No 573

Note that this index only gives the year and quarter of the death. An image of the original record would have the exact date of death, profession of the deceased, cause of death, name of the informant, relation of the informant to the deceased, etc.

In some cases an image of the original record will not be available, and the information in the index is all that you will have. It is advisable to check the image, if available, because the image may have valuable information not contained in the index, and because there can be mistakes in the index, where information has been transcribed from the record incorrectly.


Indexes to the Civil Records of Irish Births, Deaths and Marriages

The Indexes to the Civil Records of Irish Births, Deaths and Marriages date from 1864 with non-Roman Catholic Marriages recorded from 1845.

The years covered by the release of the historic Indexes to the Civil Records of Births, Marriages and Deaths are:

Births : 1864 to 1915
Marriages : 1845* to 1940
Deaths : 1864 to 1965

*non-Roman Catholic Marriages are recorded from 1845, Roman Catholic Marriages are recorded from 1864


Register images of the Civil Records of Irish Births, Deaths and Marriages

The years covered by the release of the historic records of Births, Marriages and Deaths are:

Births : 1864 to 1916
Marriages : 1870** to 1941
Deaths : 1878** to 1966

**The General Register Office are currently working on updating further records of Marriages dating back to 1845 and Deaths dating back to 1864. These will be included in future updates to the records available on the website, currently expected mid-2018.
