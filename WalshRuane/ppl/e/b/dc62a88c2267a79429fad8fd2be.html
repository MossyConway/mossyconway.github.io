        Descriptive Particulars

        These Lands are situate within two-and-a-half miles of Claremorris, a Post, Fair, and Market Town, fifteen and-a-half miles of Tuam, and twenty-nine miles of Athenry, where there is a Station of the Midland Great Railway.
        The soil consists of a warm, friable loam - well adapted for green crop, tillage, or a sheep-walk. Part is light and shallow; but could be much improved by deep tillage, manuring and liming - all of which could economically done, as the lands are free from rocks or stones; amd being warm and dry can be labored at any season; and the expensive process of draining required on other soils is unnecessary.
