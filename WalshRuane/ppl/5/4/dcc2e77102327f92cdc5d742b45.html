Event Note

Austin's original destination was his cousin Delia Flanagan 14 Rice Street, Brookline MA, but this destination was lined out and 1131 7th Avenue NY written in.

He was travelling on the same ship as his cousin Thomas Flanagan (born 1889) who was also specified Delia as his destination, but the address Thomas gave was 16 Price Street. Thomas destination was not modified.

Narrative

"There is some history that he may have made himself to be younger as he went to England during the first war and knocking off 2 years may have avoided the Draft?? There are stories that he was drafted and my grandfather made some accommodation to get him out. "No son of mine will “ware” a British uniform" - Dennis Cleary

Narrative

"Nora (Hanorah) she was sponsored (as immigrant to the U.S.) by her uncle, William Mannion, Peter was her father. Of interest she was a servant in the Vanderbilt household and spent a few summers at the Breakers in Newport. She came to the States to raise enough money to get married to the son of a neighbor in Mayo Abbey. However, my dad noticed a photograph of her on display in a photo studio an persuaded the owner to make an introduction. " - Dennis Cleary
