Narrative

"Tim was trained for the priesthood from 1891 to 1898 at Carlow College, Co. Carlow and ordained on 4 (or 14) June 1898. Following his emigration to Australia he served in Burrowa as assistant until 1905 and temporary charge of Burrowa in 1906. He then was appointed Administrator at Goulbum Cathedral for the 1907-1909 period, and later Pastor Henty until 1916. He then had a very short assignment in Gunning, interrupted by his death at Calley, Queensland sent there to recover from a bout of pneumonia contracted in making a sick call. His remains were brought back for a Requiem at Goulburn on 22 May 1917 and burial in the nearby cemetery at Kenmore. Death date on his tombstone reads either 16th or 19th of May at age 47." - James Thomas Cleary
