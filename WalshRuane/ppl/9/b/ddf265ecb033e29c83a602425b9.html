        James later married Anne Eileen Scott on 30 July 1919 at St. Francis R.C. Church in Melbourne. She was born 1 Aug 1888 in Rochester, Victoria. Ann Eileen died on 6 Jan 1970 in Geelong and was buried with James in Coborg, Victoria.
