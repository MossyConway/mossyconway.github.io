Narrative

There is a record of a Thomas P. Ruane or Brother Michael of Christian Bros. La Salle from Claremorris travelling from Montreal to Buffalo, NY in1927. This person has a number of attributes in common with Thomas Ruane in this tree, name, place of birth, age and occupation. However there are some details that suggest that this record does refer to the Thomas Patrick Ruane in this tree.

The form says that he was not in the U.S. previously. Thomas Ruane in this tree migrated to the U.S. in 1906.
The form says that his eyes were blue. Thomas Ruane's WWI Registartion Card and his immigration information in 1936 say that his eyes were brown.
The form says that he was 5'8". Thomas Ruane's WWI Registartion Card says that he was "Tall" and his immigration information in 1936 say that he was 5'11".
