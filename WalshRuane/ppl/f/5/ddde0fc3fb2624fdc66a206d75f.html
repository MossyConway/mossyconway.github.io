Narrative

"Nora (Hanorah) she was sponsored (as immigrant to the U.S.) by her uncle, William Mannion, Peter was her father. Of interest she was a servant in the Vanderbilt household and spent a few summers at the Breakers in Newport. She came to the States to raise enough money to get married to the son of a neighbor in Mayo Abbey. However, my dad noticed a photograph of her on display in a photo studio an persuaded the owner to make an introduction. " - Dennis Cleary
