Death

Date 1939-12-00

Narrative

No source reference for this but apparently: Martin J. died on December 6, 1939 in St. Louis. His Death Certificate listed his birth as March 1, 1863. Martin was listed as a retired motorman, the widower of Mary, living at 3851 Utah Place. That is the address that his daughter Virginia and her family were living at in the 1940 census. His father is noted as John Cleary and mother unknown, both of Ireland. His son, J. Leo Cleary reported the information.
