Property

Date from 1877 to 1912
Place Cossallagh, Culnacleha, Mayo, Ireland
Description Lot 8

Narrative

In 1877, James took over the land of Thomas Cleary b.1828 in Lot 8, . At this time, then, Lot 8 is jointly held between Thomas Cleary and Martin Cleary. Around 1912-1914 James Cleary remained the holder of his property in lot #8 of Cossallagh, but the land had been rearranged through government action. The lot number was changed and his land rearranged so that it also include the large house which formerly belonged to his nephew, Michael Cleary, the son of the land’s 1856 owner, Martin Cleary. James remained there until his death.
