  2. South Mayo Family Research Centre & Jim Fritch: Cleary Family History Report
      • Page: Lot 8 Cosssallagh Martin Cleary to Michael Cleary and then to unrelated parties
      • Source text:

        In the early revisions of the Griffith Valuation of 1856, Martin Cleary, the father of Michael held Lot #8 (a joint holding with Patrick Cleary) of Cossallagh. Martin Cleary died in 1892. The property revisions of 1893 indicate that Martin’s land passed to his first son, Michael. Michael and his family remained in Cossallagh until they moved to Lugboy Demense by 1911, at which time the land was distributed to unrelated parties.
