Narrative

Why are Mary Cusack's Foley family not included in this tree?

The tree I received from Jeremiah Russell has Mary Cusack of Trough, Clare married to a Foley (first name not known) with the following children:

Jimmy
Bridget
Hannie
Mary Anne b1873

The 1901 census has a Foley family in Lickadoon, Roxborough, Limerick with children whose names are James (26), Hannie (25) and Bridget (24). The father Thomas is a widower. There is no Mary Anne in the household, however there is a record of Mary Anne Foley of Lickadoon, whose father was Thomas, marrying in 1895.

There is a record of Thomas and Mary marrying in 1868, and of Mary dying in 1878, the year after Bridget was born.

There are records of the children being born in Lickadoon in the 1870s to Thomas and Mary Foley, in the same order as they appear in the census (plus Mary Anne in 1873).

It seems likely that the Foley family in the tree is the same family in the census. However Mary Foley's maiden name in these records is Blackwell, not Cusack. Assuming that Mary did not change her name from Cusack to Blackwell, it would seem that this Foley family and their decendants should not be connected to Mary Cusack. Without further information it is impossible to know if Mary Cusack was married to a Foley or not.
