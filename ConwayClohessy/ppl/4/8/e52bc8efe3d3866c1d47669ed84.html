        Obituary

        Helen R. Hannan Lepper, 98 of Attleboro, a loving mother, grandmother and great-grandmother, passed away peacefully surrounded by her family on Friday, January 21st at the LifeCare Center of Attleboro. She was the beloved wife of the late Thomas J. Hannan Jr. and the late John E. Lepper.

        Born Helen R. Phelan in Attleboro on February 12, 1912 Helen was the daughter of the late John and Louise (Gaffney) Phelan. She was raised and educated in Attleboro and was a graduate of Attleboro High School.

        Mrs. Lepper was employed as a Social Worker at the Attleboro Department of Public Welfare for 25 years before retiring in 1977. Mrs. Lepper was an avid reader and enjoyed spending time with her family, especially her great grandchildren.
