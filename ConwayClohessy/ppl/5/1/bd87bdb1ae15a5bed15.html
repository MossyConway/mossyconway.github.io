Narrative

Patrick and Jane registered the birth of a Patrick Halpin with a birth date of 22nd May 1864 .

https://civilrecords.irishgenealogy.ie/churchrecords/details-civil/7d824f4670580

Considering that there does not appear to be a record of Patrick's baptism, or a record of Mary Agnes' birth registration, it seems likely that Patrick's birth registration and Mary Agnes' baptism refer to the same child, meaning that Mary Agnes was erroneously recorded as Patrick in the birth register.

Note that it was not unusual for births to be registered as happening later that they really happened in order for the parents to avoid a fine for late registration.
