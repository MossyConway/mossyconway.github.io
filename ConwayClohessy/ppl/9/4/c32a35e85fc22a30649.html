Narrative

In the 1920 US Census Sadie Perry is married to Keane Matthew Ambrose .
In the 1930 US Census Sadie Perry is a widow.

In August 1939 after Hannie Ambroses' death, Frannie Ambrose placed a notice in The Brooklyn Daily Eagle relating to Hannie Ambroses' will. The notice addressed several people, but the wording used to address Keane Ambrose suggested that it was not known if he was alive or dead, however an obituary of Hannie Ambroses' names Keane as a surviving child.

In April 1942 a World War II registration card was filled out in the name of Michael Keene Ambrose , with an address of 343 E. 26th St., Brooklyn, New York which is the family home of the Ambroses' in this tree. He gives Nora Dillon as a contact. His date of birth is given as 27th August 1882. Keane Matthew Ambrose gave his date of birth as 27th August 1884 on his World War I registration card in 1918.

There are no records of Michael Keene Ambrose living with the Ambrose family as a child in the US Census.

All of the above suggests to me that Keane Matthew Ambrose went missing in the 1920's, was declared legally dead, and reappeared sometime between August 1939 and April 1942 as Michael Keene Ambrose .

It's possible that they are two individuals, but the fact that both give 27th August as their date of birth suggests that they are the same person.
