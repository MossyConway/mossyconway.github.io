Mount St. Lawrence Burial Ground Registers

Author Limerick City and County Council
Publication information http://archives.limerick.ie/index.php/Register/Search/Index

Narrative

The records contain the names, addresses at time of death, ages, position of the grave and dates of death of all those buried in the cemetery.

The registers can be searched here: http://archives.limerick.ie/index.php/Register/Search/Index

Images of the original burial register books can be browed here: https://www.limerick.ie/historicalresources/limerickarchives/cemeteryrecords/burialgroundregisters/mountstlawrenceburialgroundregisters1855-2008

Note that some records cannot be found via the search engine, those records can only found via the second link above, by reading the original hand-written records.
