Author Board of Admiralty, Royal Marines
Publication information http://discovery.nationalarchives.gov.uk/details/r/C1868

Narrative

Reference: ADM 159

Title: Admiralty: Royal Marines: Registers of Service

Description:
These registers of Royal Marines' service contain, as well as information similar to that contained in attestation papers, a description of physical characteristics at the time of re-engagement and discharge together with full details of service.
Digital copies of records of those who joined the Royal Marines between 1842 and 1936 can be searched and downloaded.
Note: Referencing: In 2012, as part of transferring digitised records to The National Archives' new Discovery catalogue, the items throughout this series were temporarily numbered, starting at 1 within each piece. In March 2013 these were corrected to have their item references constructed of the piece number followed by a slash and the numerical part of the register number; for example ADM 159/5/1 is now ADM 159/5/2549.

Date: 1842-1936

Arrangement:
Entries are arranged in register number order within divisions (e.g. Royal Marines Light Infantry, Chatham, or Royal Marines Band) but the dates noted in the catalogue give an approximate indication of the enlistment years covered in each register volume.
