FRIDAY, 28 AUGUST, 2020

A Hannan memoir

I received a copy of a memoir written by Thomas Hannan [1874 - 1964], thanks to Hugh Sweeney. Thomas was first-cousin-once-removed to Thomas Conway [1900 - 1980] and his sister Annie was married to Thomas Cusack [1863 - 1925] who was an uncle of Bridie Clohessy [1904 - 1986]. The memoir details Thomas's life growing up in Limerick and Dublin, and he names many of his relatives and where they lived. I have used this information in combination with baptism and land records to find the parents of Bridget Hannan [1830 - 1903] and her aunts, uncles and other relatives. The memoir can be found in the Download section.
