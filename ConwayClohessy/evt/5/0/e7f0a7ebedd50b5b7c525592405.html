        HANNAN (Rita) (née Madden) (St Enda's, Moycullen, Co. Galway) (former Chairman Galway Rural Development Company Ltd) - September 17, 2005 (peacefully) in Beaumont Hospital, Dublin. Sadly missed by her husband Eamonn, sons John, Eamon, Kieran and Enda, sisters Claire, Noreen and Laura, daughters-in-law, brothers-in-law, sisters-in-law, cousins, nieces, nephews and a large circle of friends. Remains will be removed from Beaumont Hospital today (Monday) at 3 o'clock to arrive at Moycullen Church at 8 o'clock (approx.) Funeral tomorrow (Tuesday) after 12 o'clock Mass to the adjoining cemetery. House private.May she rest in peace.
