        Kathleen H. “Kathy” Wolf, 59, of VanCurler Avenue, Schenectady, New York; an Attleboro native; died on Wednesday, November 9, 2005 at Columbia Memorial Hospital in Hudson, New York following a long illness. She was the wife of Dwight S. Wolf to whom she had been married for 36 years.

        Born in Attleboro, Massachusetts on May 7, 1946, she was the beloved daughter of John and Alice (Jette) Hannan of Attleboro. She was raised and educated in Attleboro where she was a graduate of Attleboro High School and earned her bachelor’s degree in education from the former Framingham State Teacher College.

        She moved to Schenectady in 1977 and had been employed as a substitute teacher with the Schenectady Public School System. She was a volunteer Den Mother and often donated her time at the Schenectady County Public Library. She was a communicant of St. John the Evangelist Church both in Attleboro and Schenectady.

        In addition to her husband and parents, she leaves a son: Sean T. Wolf of Hilton Head Island, South Carolina; a sister: Patricia Clement of New London, NH; a brother: Peter Hannan of Guilford, Vermont and several nieces and nephews. She was the sister of the late John Hannan.

        A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Monday, November 14, 2005 at 9:30 AM at St. John the Evangelist Church, Schenectady, New York.

        Relatives and friends are cordially invited to attend a graveside service on Tuesday, November 15, 2005 at 10:00 AM at St. John’s Cemetery, West Street, Attleboro, with the Rev. Monsignor Daniel F. Hoye, Pastor of St. John the Evangelist Roman Catholic Church, Attleboro, officiating. Burial will follow.
