        POLICE FORCE.
        NEW SUPERINTENDENT.
        Superintendent Michael Hannan, who
        was recently promoted from the rank of
        Inspector to that of Superintendent,
        was born In Limerick, Ireland, on Au-
        gust 21, 1851. He came to Victoria
        when about 14 years of age.
        In 1871 he joined the Victorian Per-
        manent Artillery, and was subsequently
        transferred to the police force. He was
        made senior constable in 1890, and, after
        being stationed In various parts of the
        State, Including South Yarra and Col-
        llngwood, was promoted to the rank of
        sub-inspector in 1901. The next step
        to an inspectorship was made In 1907.
        He was placed In charge of tho Ben-
        digo district, where he has remained up
        to the present time.
        While stationed as a constable at
        Warrnambool, Superintendent Hannan
        saved a man from drowning, and was
        presented with the Royal Humane So-
        ciety's certificate and bronze medal.
        Superintendent Hannan will retire from
        the service on August 21 next.
