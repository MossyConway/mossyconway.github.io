Residence

Place Gerald Griffin Street, Limerick, Limerick, Ireland
Description Previously known as Cornwallis Street

Narrative

This property, 28 Cornwallis Street, was passed down to Tom from his father Michael who had been there since at least as far back a the original Valuation Office publication in 1850, so Tom may have been there all his life.

There was a Hannan family living at 2 Cornwallis Street, though I have not seen evidence connecting them to the Hannans in this tree. Thomas Hannan and several of relatives have lived in 28 Cornwallis Street, so records referring to Hannans living in Cornwallis Street are sometimes relevant, but some of those records refer to the "wrong" Hannans.
