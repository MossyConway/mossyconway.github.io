        HANNAN Eamonn - January 21, 2011 ('St. Enda's', Moycullen, Co. Galway, Pointe Boise Nursing Home, Salthill, and late of Limerick) (unexpectedly) in the care of the staff of St. Rita's Ward, University Hospital Galway, (former CEO of The Western Health Board, President of the Connemara Pony Breeder's Society, Chairman of Galway County Show and Chairman of Galway Bay FM). Predeceased by his beloved wife Rita, dearly loved father of John, Eamon, Kieran and Enda, grandfather to Alannah, Caoimhe and Daire, brother of Ann Noonan (Limerick), Mary Barry (Kilmallock), Margaret Carroll (Chicago) and Katha Hannan (Indiana), daughters-in-law Maria, Margaret and Janet, nieces, nephews and a wide circle of friends. Reposing this (Monday) evening at Aiséirí, Church Road, Moycullen (opposite church) between 3 o'c and 7 o'c followed by removal to The Church of the Immaculate Conception, Moycullen. Requiem Mass tomorrow (Tuesday) at 12 noon followed by burial in the adjoining Cemetery. Family flowers only please, donations in lieu to St. Rita's Ward Stroke Unit, University Hospital Galway.'Ar dheis Dé go raibh a anam dílis.'
