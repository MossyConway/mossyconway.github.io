        RE PATRICK HANNAN, Deceased.— Notice to
        Creditors. — Pursuant to the Provisions of the
        Statute of Trusts, 1874 : Notice is hereby given that
        all CREDITORS and other persons having any CLAIMS
        or demands against the estate of Patrick Hannan,
        late of Richmond, in the colony of Victoria, police
        constable, deceased, who died on the fifth day of
        April, one thousand eight hundred and seventy-flve,
        and probate of whose will was, on the eighteenth
        day of August, one thousand eight hundred and
        seventy-five, granted to PHILLIP MEADE, of the
        police-station, Carlton, in the said colony, police
        constable, and PATRICK BARRY, of the Police
        Hospital, Richmond, in the said colony, dispenser of
        medicine, the executors named in and appointed by
        the said will, are hereby required to send full parti-
        culars of such claims or demands addressed to the
        said Phillip Meade and Patrick Barry, to the
        care of Messieurs M'Kean and Wilson, num
        ber forty-three Elizabeth-street, Melbourne, in
        the said colony, solicitors, on or before the thir-
        teenth day of November, one thousand eight hundred
        and seventy-five. And notice is hereby given that
        after the said thirteenth day of November, one thou
        sand eight hundred and seventy-five, the said Phillip
        Meade and Patrick Barry will proceed to distribute
        the assets of the said Patrick Hannan, deceased,
        amongst the parties entitled thereto, having regard
        only to the claims or demands of which the said
        Phillip Meade and Patrick Barry shall then have had
        notice ; and the said Phillip Meade and Patrick Barry
        will not be liable for the assets or any part thereof so
        distributed to the persons of whose claims they shall
        not then have had notice.
        Dated the twenty-fourth day of September, A.D.
        1875.+
        M'KEAN aud WILSON, 43 Elizabeth -street, Mel
        bourne, Proctors for the executors.
