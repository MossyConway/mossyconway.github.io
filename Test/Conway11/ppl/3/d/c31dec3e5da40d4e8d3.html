Notes

Note

Information I have from Mary Looby says that Mary and John had a child called "Agnes". There is no evidence of existence in 1901 or 1911 census. There are two children in the Geary household in the 1911 Census (Julia May, and Delia Teresa), the same Census states that Mary Anne Geary had only had two children.

Perhaps "Agnes" was an alternative name for one of the two children.
