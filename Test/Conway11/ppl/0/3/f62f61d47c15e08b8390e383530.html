        DEATH OF THE REV. T. HALPIN, P.P.

        We regret to record the death of the Rev.
        Timothy Halpin, P.P., Donoughmore, which oc-
        curred yesterday afternoon at his residence, Clon-
        long House. Father Halpin, who was in his 78th
        year, had been ailing for a month, and his death
        was caused by an affection of the lungs. He had
        been Parish Priest of Donoughmore for twenty-
        two years, having been transferred from Mungret.
        He was the brother of the late Archdeacon Halpin,
        Rathkeale; Rev Jeremiah Halpin, Banogue; and
        Mr Michael Halpin, Querry Hill, Kilmallock.
        The Office and Requiem Mass will take place on
        Wednesday.
