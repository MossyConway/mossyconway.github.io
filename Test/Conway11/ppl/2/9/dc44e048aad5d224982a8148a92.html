Notes

Research

According to the ONEILL Web Site on MyHeritage (managed by Morgan O'Neill: https://www.myheritage.com/member-442739971_475318441/morgan-oneill) Johanna was born on 01 Apr 1876 in Clashalaher, Cashel . There is a civil record of Johanna O'Neill's birth at that time and place, listing her father as Michael O'Neill. However Johanna and John's marriage record shows that Johanna's father was Morgan O' Neill. It's possible that one of those records is wrong, but it seems likely that the Johanna that married John is not the Johanna born to Michael O'Neill in 1876.
