Notes

Note

Note that the Cancelled Land Books show that Patrick Halpin had replaced Michael Halpin as occupier of Lot 26 in Drombanny , Cahernarry by 1867. This corresponds with the death of Michael Halpin in 1866. Patrick remained occupier at least until 1897 when his daughter Mary Agnes married Patrick Conway.

That is an indication that Michael Halpin 1784 - 1866, who was married to Ann Peagum, could be Patrick's father.

The marriage record of Patrick's brother Michael 1830 - 1904 of Ardkilmartin shows that his father's name was also Michael.

Together this information indicates that Michael b1784 and Ann Peagum were likely the parents of Michael b1830, Patrick and their siblings.
